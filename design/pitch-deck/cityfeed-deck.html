The Problem

Physical advertising is broken

Buying a billboard shouldn't be harder than buying a Facebook ad. But today, it is.

🔒

Opaque Pricing

No public rates. You have to call, email, and negotiate just to find out what a placement costs.

🧩

Fragmented Market

Thousands of independent ad spaces — storefronts, vehicles, local billboards — with no central place to find them.

🚫

Inaccessible

Small and mid-size businesses are priced out or can't navigate the process. Hosts with empty spaces can't find buyers.

The Solution

One marketplace for every ad space

How It Works

For Hosts

Turn your space into revenue

1

List your space

Upload photos, set your location, pricing, and availability. Your listing goes live immediately.

2

Receive booking requests

Advertisers request your space — or book instantly if you enable it. You stay in control.

3

Get creative files

The advertiser uploads their artwork directly in-app. No email chains, no lost files.

4

Post the ad & upload proof

Install the creative and snap a photo as Proof of Posting (POP). This triggers payment release.

5

Get paid via Stripe

Funds are released from escrow directly to your Stripe account. Fast, secure, automatic.

How It Works

For Advertisers

Book ad space in minutes

1

Browse the marketplace

Search by location, ad type, and price. Filter by availability and see real photos of every space.

2

Book a placement

Select your campaign dates and pay securely. Your payment is held in escrow until the ad goes live.

3

Upload your creative

Send your artwork, files, or design specs directly through the platform. The host gets notified instantly.

4

Get photo proof it's live

The host uploads a Proof of Posting photo — you'll see your ad in the real world, confirmed.

5

Track your campaign

Monitor active campaigns from your dashboard. See status, dates, and proof — all in one place.
