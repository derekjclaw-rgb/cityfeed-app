# City Feed Las Vegas Launch — No Excuses Roadmap
*You have everything you need. Now execute.*

---

## WEEK 1: Prep (May 1–4)

### Day 1 (Thursday night / Friday)
- [ ] Read the sales script front to back. Memorize the opener and the 30-second pitch
- [ ] Read the prospect list. Highlight your top 10 targets (pick bars/cafes you've actually been to)
- [ ] Save the host one-pager link on your phone so you can text it instantly
- [ ] Make sure cityfeed.io loads clean on mobile — you'll be showing it to people

### Day 2 (Saturday)
- [ ] Drive through Arts District + Downtown. Recon. Look at the businesses on your list with fresh eyes
- [ ] Note which ones have obvious ad space (empty walls, bathroom frames, window space, chalkboards)
- [ ] Rank your top 10 by easiest conversation (places you already go, bartenders you know, etc.)

### Day 3 (Sunday)
- [ ] Create YOUR OWN test listing on City Feed — go through the full host flow yourself
- [ ] Screenshot each step. Know it cold. When someone says "show me," you pull it up in 5 seconds
- [ ] Practice the pitch out loud 3 times. Not in your head. Out loud.

---

## WEEK 2: First 10 Hosts (May 5–11)

### Goal: 10 signed up hosts with live listings

### Monday–Wednesday (May 5–7)
- [ ] Hit 3 businesses per day. That's it. Just 3.
- [ ] Walk in, ask for the manager/owner, deliver the pitch
- [ ] If they're interested: help them list on the spot OR text them the one-pager
- [ ] If they say "not now": get their number, text the one-pager, follow up in 48 hours
- [ ] Log every conversation in your phone notes: name, venue, reaction, follow-up date

### Thursday–Friday (May 8–9)
- [ ] Follow up with everyone from Mon–Wed who didn't sign up yet
- [ ] Text: "Hey [Name], Michael from City Feed. Still want to get that [bar top/bathroom/window] listed? Takes 2 min — I can walk you through it on the phone"
- [ ] Hit 2 more new businesses each day

### Weekend (May 10–11)
- [ ] Check your dashboard. How many listings are live?
- [ ] Help any hosts who started but didn't finish their listing
- [ ] Take photos of listed spaces (offer the free photography from founding host offer)
- [ ] Upload photos to their listings for them if needed

### Week 2 Checkpoint:
**You should have 10 live listings.** If you don't, you didn't talk to enough people. Go back out Monday.

---

## WEEK 3: Density + First Demand (May 12–18)

### Goal: 25 live listings + first advertiser inquiry

### Monday–Wednesday
- [ ] Keep hitting new businesses — target 3/day
- [ ] Focus on CLUSTERS. If you got 2 bars on Fremont East, get the other 3 on that same block
- [ ] Start posting on Instagram/social: "City Feed is live in Downtown Vegas" with photos of listed spaces

### Thursday–Friday
- [ ] Reach into your nightlife network. Text 5 people who work with brands/promoters:
  - "Hey, I built something you might want to use — marketplace for real-world ad placements in Vegas. Got 25 spaces downtown. Want me to send you the link?"
- [ ] Follow up with all pending hosts

### Weekend
- [ ] Review all listings — fix any with bad photos or weak descriptions
- [ ] Use the listing templates to upgrade descriptions
- [ ] Take more photos if needed

### Week 3 Checkpoint:
**25 live listings. At least 1 inbound advertiser question.** If no advertiser interest yet, your listings need better photos or you need to start seeding demand yourself.

---

## WEEK 4: First Transactions (May 19–25)

### Goal: 5 completed bookings

### Monday–Tuesday
- [ ] Personally reach out to 5 potential advertisers:
  - Local event promoters
  - Restaurant/bar owners who want to cross-promote
  - Your LIV/nightlife brand contacts who have upcoming events
  - Anyone who's ever asked "how do I advertise in Vegas?"
- [ ] Send them the product deck + direct links to relevant listings

### Wednesday–Thursday
- [ ] If no bookings yet: CREATE DEMAND YOURSELF
  - Book a placement on your own platform as a test advertiser (use a different email)
  - This proves the full flow works AND gives hosts their first booking experience
  - Screenshot the host's payout confirmation — use it as social proof for other hosts

### Friday–Sunday
- [ ] Follow up with every advertiser lead
- [ ] Check in with hosts who got bookings — how was the experience?
- [ ] Ask happy hosts: "Know anyone else who'd want to list their space?"

### Week 4 Checkpoint:
**5 completed bookings. Real money moved. At least 1 host referral.**

---

## WEEK 5–6: Flywheel (May 26 – June 8)

### Goal: 50 listings, 10+ transactions, 2–3 repeat advertisers

- [ ] Double down on what's working. Which business type converts easiest? Do more of those.
- [ ] Start bundling: "Book 5 bar tops downtown for $XX/week" — campaign packages
- [ ] Ask every happy host and advertiser for a testimonial (screenshot or quote)
- [ ] Post results on social: "City Feed hosts earned $X this month from space they weren't using"
- [ ] Start Tier 2 supply: apartment elevators, boutique retail, wild postings, event venues

---

## Daily Non-Negotiables

Every single day during launch:

1. **Talk to at least 2 new businesses** (even if it's a 30-second intro)
2. **Follow up with 3 pending leads** (text, not email — faster response)
3. **Check the dashboard** — new signups? New bookings? Anything broken?
4. **Post something on social** — photo of a listed space, a win, a behind-the-scenes moment
5. **Update Derek** — tell me what happened, what's working, what's not. I'll fix product issues same-day.
