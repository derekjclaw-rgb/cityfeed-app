## Notes for Sales Team

### Priority Targets (High Foot Traffic + Good Fit)
- **Esther's Kitchen** — Anchor of Arts District, massive customer base
- **Velveteen Rabbit** — Arts District icon, creative crowd
- **Makers & Finders** — Daily coffee traffic, community hub
- **Able Baker Brewing** — Local brewery staple, sidewalk spill-out
- **Commonwealth** — High-volume nightlife, rooftop bar
- **Oak & Ivy** — Fremont East hub, outdoor seating
- **Shanghai Taste** — James Beard-nominated, always packed
- **Chops Barbershop DTLV** — Popular barbershop + vintage clothing store
- **Bungalow Coffee Co** — High daily foot traffic, workspace crowd

### Neighborhood Notes
- **Downtown/Fremont East:** Best for nightlife-oriented placements. High weekend foot traffic. Bars cluster along E Fremont St between Las Vegas Blvd and 8th St.
- **Arts District (18b):** Densest cluster of independent businesses. Main St between Charleston and Wyoming is the spine. First Friday events bring massive crowds monthly. Mix of day (coffee/restaurants) and night (bars/clubs) traffic.
- **Chinatown:** Spring Mountain Rd from Valley View to Rainbow (~3 miles). Primarily restaurant/food-oriented. Strong lunch and dinner traffic. Boba shops attract younger demographic. Less nightlife but heavy food tourism.

### Gaps to Fill (Future Research)
- Dispensaries/smoke shops (several exist but need specific verification)
- Laundromats (sparse in these neighborhoods)
- Food trucks (First Friday has many; need individual operator contacts)
- Boutique retail shops (scattered through Arts District — need field verification)
- Hair salons/nail bars (several in Chinatown strip malls)
