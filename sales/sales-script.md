# City Feed — Host Sales Script
*For founder-led, in-person conversations*

---

## The Opener (10 seconds)

**Walk in, look around, identify the ad space.**

> "Hey, I'm Michael — I run a company called City Feed. We help businesses like yours earn passive income from ad space you already have. Got 60 seconds?"

---

## The Pitch (30 seconds)

> "See that [bar top / bathroom wall / window / counter space]? Brands will pay to put their ads there. We handle everything — the marketplace, the payments, the matching. You list your space, advertisers book it, you get paid directly to your bank account."

---

## The Hook (10 seconds)

> "We're launching in Downtown Vegas right now and offering founding hosts zero fees for the first 30 days. Free photography of your space too."

---

## If They're Interested

> "It takes about 2 minutes to list. You pick your price — most [bars/cafes/shops] in the area are listing at $1 to $5 a day. That's $30 to $150 a month from space that's making you nothing right now."

> "Here — I'll text you the link. You can set it up on your phone tonight or I can help you do it right now."

**→ Get their phone number. Text them the one-pager link + signup URL.**

---

## Common Objections

**"What kind of ads?"**
> "You approve everything before it goes up. Nothing goes on your wall that you haven't said yes to. You're in full control."

**"How do I get paid?"**
> "Direct deposit through Stripe — same system Shopify and Uber use. Money hits your account within a few days of the ad going up."

**"What if nobody books my space?"**
> "It's free to list. You only pay a small fee when you actually earn money. Zero risk."

**"I don't have time for this."**
> "The listing takes 2 minutes. After that, we handle everything — you just approve bookings and collect money. Literally passive income."

**"How much can I actually make?"**
> "Depends on the space, but a single bar top placement at $3/day is $90 a month. Most hosts list multiple spaces. One bar downtown is listing 4 spots and pulling in $300+ a month."

**"I need to ask my manager/owner."**
> "Totally get it. I'll text you a one-pager you can forward to them. Takes 30 seconds to read."

---

## The Close

> "Want me to help you set it up right now? Takes 2 minutes."

OR

> "Let me text you the link — you can do it whenever. What's your number?"

**Always leave with:**
1. Their phone number
2. A text sent with the one-pager link
3. Their name (for follow-up)

---

## Follow-Up (Next Day)

> "Hey [Name], Michael from City Feed. Great meeting you yesterday at [venue]. Here's your listing link if you haven't set it up yet: cityfeed.io/dashboard/create-listing — let me know if you need a hand. We're featuring founding hosts on the homepage this month."

---

## Key Principles

- **Don't oversell.** The product is simple. Keep the conversation simple.
- **Show, don't tell.** Pull up the site on your phone. Show them a listing. Show them the dashboard.
- **Make it about THEIR space.** "See that bathroom? That's $3 a day."
- **Remove all friction.** Offer to set it up for them on the spot.
- **Founding host scarcity.** "We're only taking 50 founding hosts in this area."
- **Follow up within 24 hours.** Every time.
